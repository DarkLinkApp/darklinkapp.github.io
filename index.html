How to Backup?

01

In Link Messenger, go to Settings > Chats > Chat backups.

02

Tap on Local Backup, then click Enable.

03

Select a folder (it is recommended to create a separate folder beforehand).

04

Copy the passphrase generated and write it down safely. You will need this!

05

After enabling, click Create Backup. Verify that the backup file is present in your chosen folder.

06

Open DarkLink, register your number, select Restore Backup, choose the file and enter code.

FAQ

Common questions about DarkLink

Why switch from Link Messenger to DarkLink?
No need unless you want to see who visited your profile without premium, or keep deleted messages.
"Delete for Everyone" doesn't work for me?
When you delete for everyone in the mod, it is deleted for the recipient, but stays for you. Use "Delete for Me" to remove it from your view.
Is it safe? Any malware?
It's your choice. However, I guarantee no malicious code was added, only the features listed on the site.
Will there be future updates?
Yes! We plan to add new features and adapt to official Link Messenger updates.
How to activate Ghost Mode?
Go to Settings > Privacy > Last Seen & Online. Turn it off. Now incoming messages will stay as one tick for the sender, even if you are online.
Does Anti-delete work for stories?
Yes, it prevents both messages and contact stories from being deleted on your end.
